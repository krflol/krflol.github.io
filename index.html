PrepperMap Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10/12/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to PrepperMap. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location data (when you grant permission)
  • Photos (when you choose to add them to inventory items)
  • Inventory and maintenance task information you input into the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our mapping and inventory management services
  • Save and display your markers, inventory items, and maintenance tasks
  • Customize your app experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal information on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Maps API for mapping functionality. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
